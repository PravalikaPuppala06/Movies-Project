Nanna

Krishna (Vikram) who has the mental faculties of a five-year-old works in a
chocolate factory. His wife delivers a baby girl and dies. Krishna calls her
Vennela (Sarah) and she becomes the centre of his world. He soon admits her
in a school, where Vennela befriends the school correspondent (Amala Paul)

List of characters:

  1. Vikram as Krishna
  2. Sara as Nila (daughter of Vikram)
  3. Anushka shetty as lawyer
  4. Amala paul as a teacher(sister of vikram's wife)
