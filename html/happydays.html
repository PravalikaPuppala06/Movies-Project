Happy Days

Happy Days tells us a story that explores the lives of eight friends through the four
years of their engineering course.They bond over as they go through bullying seniors,
stringent professors, intense examinations, over the top celebrations,love, betrayal,
sacrifice and every other possible emotion that an individual can experience in a college.

List of characters:

  2. Nikhil Siddharth as Rajesh
  3. Gayatri Rao as Aparna "Appu"
  4. Rahul Haridas as Arjun "Tyson"
  5. Sonia Deepti as Shravanthi "Shravs"
  6. Vamsee Chaganti as Shankar
  7. Monali Chowdhary as Sangeetha
  8. Gururaj Manepalli as College Principal
  9. Kamalinee Mukherjee as Shreya Madam
  10. Randhir Gattla as Naveen
  11. Aadarsh Balakrishna as Sanjay
